- community-driven
- long-term sustainability

## Structural Patterns

### Content Tells

- **Significance inflation**: Inflating routine events into history-making ones ("watershed moment for the industry").
- **Notability name-dropping**: Piling on prestigious citations ("cited in NYT, BBC, and Wired") without context.
- **Superficial -ing analyses**: Strings of present participles ("symbolizing progress, reflecting investment").
- **Promotional language**: Tourism-brochure prose ("nestled within the breathtaking foothills").
- **Vague attributions**: "Experts believe," "Studies show" without naming sources.
- **Formulaic challenges**: "Despite challenges, continues to thrive."
- **Novelty inflation**: Treating established concepts as if the speaker invented them.

### Language Tells

- **Copula avoidance**: Substituting "is" and "has" with "serves as," "features," "boasts," etc.
- **Synonym cycling**: Conspicuously rotating synonyms (developers... engineers... practitioners... builders).
- **Template phrases**: "a [adj] step towards [adj] infrastructure."
- **Filler phrases**: "In order to," "Due to the fact that," "It's worth noting that."
- **False ranges**: Pairing unrelated extremes ("from the Big Bang to dark matter").
- **Parenthetical hedging**: Inserting parenthetical asides to sound nuanced without committing.

### Structural Tells

- **Uniformity**: Metronomic sentence and paragraph length.
- **Formulaic openings**: Broad context before the point ("In the rapidly evolving world of...").
- **Inline-header lists**: Bullet items starting with a bold header that repeats itself.
- **Numbered list inflation**: Padding to hit a specific number of takeaways.
- **Title case headings**: Over-capitalizing subheadings.
- **Chatbot artifacts**: "Certainly!", "I hope this helps!", "Let's dive in!".
- **Hashtag stuffing**: 6+ trailing hashtags on a social post.
- **Bullet lists of bare noun phrases**: 5+ short adjective-plus-noun items with no verbs.

## Fingerprints

- **Unfilled placeholders**: `[Your Name]`, `2025-XX-XX`.
- **Chatbot citation markup**: `citeturn0search0`, `oai_citation`.
- **AI-tool URL parameters**: `utm_source=chatgpt.com`.
- **Hedge-stacked predictions**: "could potentially create," "may eventually unlock."
- **Real/actual adjective inflation**: "real on-chain tokenomics," "actual reward sustainability."
